About Nose Gambit

I'm Bibek from Nepal, the creator of Nose Gambit and Chess with Bibu.

I live with a physical disability that makes it difficult for me to walk or use my hands, so I play chess using only my nose. What many people see as a limitation, I chose to turn into my greatest strength.

Chess is more than a game for me — it is proof that determination can defeat obstacles. Through my YouTube channel, I share my chess journey, challenges, wins, losses, and the message that passion is stronger than circumstances.

Nose Gambit is not just about chess; it's about resilience, courage, and inspiring people to believe that no dream is too far, no matter the situation.
