Why It Matters

More than a channel

Playing chess without the use of my hands or legs means every video takes longer to plan, record, and edit than it might for anyone else. Camera angles, equipment, and even sitting comfortably in front of a board all take extra thought and extra time.

None of that has stopped me — and it never will. But a little support from people who believe in what Nose Gambit stands for goes a long way toward better equipment, more consistent uploads, and more room to focus on chess instead of logistics.

Whatever you're able to give, however small, genuinely helps — and it never goes unnoticed. Thank you for being part of this game.
